How to watch

Viewing Order and Complete Screen Checklist

The core order below follows the in-universe timeline. Anthology shows are marked because some episodes jump around. Current as June 5, 2026, checked against the official StarWars.com movies and series viewing guide.

Best First-Time Path

Start with release order if you have never seen Star Wars: Episodes IV-VI, I-III, then VII-IX, adding Rogue One and Solo after the prequels. This preserves the original reveals and lets the filmmaking evolve naturally.

Use chronological order when you want the story as galactic history. That is the order below, with shows placed where they fit.

Clone Wars Tip

The Clone Wars was released out of timeline order. For the cleanest story, watch the 2008 movie, then the series using the official chronological episode order before moving into Revenge of the Sith and The Bad Batch.

The Tales anthology shows can be watched after you know the characters they focus on, even though their episodes occur across multiple eras.

01

The Acolyte

High Republic mystery set long before the Skywalker saga, when the Jedi are still powerful and public.

02

Young Jedi Adventures

High Republic animated series for younger viewers, useful for seeing Jedi training before the prequel era.

03

Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi

Anthology shorts focused mainly on Ahsoka Tano and Count Dooku. Episodes span years, so treat it as character context.

04

The Phantom Menace

Episode I. Introduces Anakin, Padme, Naboo, the Senate crisis, and the Sith's return.

05

Attack of the Clones

Episode II. Sets up the Clone Wars, Anakin and Padme's secret relationship, and the Separatist conflict.

06

The Clone Wars Movie

Animated theatrical film that introduces Ahsoka as Anakin's Padawan.

07

The Clone Wars Series

Seven-season animated war epic covering clone identity, Mandalore, Maul, Ahsoka, politics, and Jedi compromise.

08

Revenge of the Sith

Episode III. Anakin falls, Order 66 destroys the Jedi, and the Empire replaces the Republic.

09

Tales of the Empire and Tales of the Underworld

Anthology shorts best watched after the prequels and Clone Wars because they deepen Imperial and criminal-side characters.

10

The Bad Batch

Clone Force 99 survives the Empire's first years as clones are replaced, hunted, and experimented on.

11

Maul - Shadow Lord

Animated Maul series set after the Clone Wars, bridging dark side survival and underworld power.

12

Solo: A Star Wars Story

Han, Chewbacca, Lando, the Falcon, and the Imperial-era underworld.

13

Obi-Wan Kenobi

Limited series about Obi-Wan's exile, Leia's childhood, and Vader's unresolved bond with his former master.

14

Andor

Cassian's radicalization, Mon Mothma's secret funding, Imperial prisons, surveillance, and early rebellion networks.

15

Star Wars Rebels

The Ghost crew, Lothal, Ezra, Kanan, Hera, Sabine, Thrawn, and the Rebel Alliance coming together.

16

Rogue One: A Star Wars Story

The mission to steal the Death Star plans. Leads directly into A New Hope.

17

A New Hope

Episode IV. Luke joins Leia, Han, Chewbacca, Obi-Wan, and the droids against the Death Star.

18

The Empire Strikes Back

Episode V. Hoth, Dagobah, Cloud City, Yoda's training, and Vader's reveal.

19

Return of the Jedi

Episode VI. Jabba's palace, Endor, the second Death Star, and Anakin's redemption.

20

The Mandalorian

Din Djarin and Grogu in the New Republic frontier. Watch seasons 1-2 before The Book of Boba Fett.

21

The Book of Boba Fett

Boba's Tatooine rule and important Mandalorian/Grogu chapters between The Mandalorian seasons 2 and 3.

22

The Mandalorian Season 3

Mandalore, Bo-Katan, the Darksaber, Grogu's growth, and Imperial remnant threads.

23

Ahsoka

Ahsoka, Sabine, Hera, Ezra, Thrawn, Nightsister ties, and New Republic blind spots.

24

Skeleton Crew

New Republic-era adventure about lost children, hidden worlds, and pirate danger.

25

The Mandalorian and Grogu

Theatrical continuation of Din and Grogu's New Republic-era story, released May 22, 2026.

26

Star Wars Resistance

Animated series that starts before The Force Awakens and continues through the sequel-era conflict.

27

The Force Awakens

Episode VII. Rey, Finn, Poe, BB-8, Kylo Ren, Starkiller Base, and the First Order's open war.

28

The Last Jedi

Episode VIII. Rey and Luke, Kylo's ambition, Resistance survival, and the meaning of Jedi legacy.

29

The Rise of Skywalker

Episode IX. Exegol, Palpatine's final design, Ben's redemption, and Rey's chosen identity.

30

Starfighter

Upcoming theatrical film set after The Rise of Skywalker; officially dated May 28, 2027, so keep it on the future list for now.
