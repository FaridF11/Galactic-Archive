The Unknown Regions

The Unknown Regions sit beyond the familiar galactic routes, where unstable hyperspace lanes, gravity anomalies, ancient dangers, and poor mapping make travel difficult. They matter because several secret powers hide there, including the Sith Eternal on Exegol, the First Order's buildup, and the Chiss Ascendancy's guarded territory.

Why they are hard to reach
Routes shift, navigation data is rare, and many paths require local knowledge or powerful Force guidance.
Why they change the story
They let threats grow outside normal politics, then return suddenly with fleets, armies, or old Force secrets.
Key examples
Exegol, Ilum as Starkiller Base, Csilla, Peridea, and the hidden worlds tied to ancient routes.
