The Canon Eras

This page keeps the big timeline separate from the rest of the archive, so you can understand where every story belongs before jumping into characters, media, or lore.

1

Dawn of the Jedi

The earliest known age of Force study, before the Jedi Order becomes the institution seen in the films. It establishes ancient temples, first teachings, and the idea that Force traditions are older than galactic governments.

2

The Old Republic

A legendary period of Jedi and Sith wars, ancient republics, lost civilizations, and traditions remembered through ruins, holocrons, and myths. Canon often references it as deep history rather than fully dramatizing it.

3

The High Republic

The Jedi operate near their cultural height. The Republic expands into the Outer Rim, the Jedi are public guardians, and threats such as the Nihil expose the limits of idealism, infrastructure, and trust.

4

Fall of the Jedi

The prequel era. Senator Palpatine engineers crisis, war, and fear. The Clone Wars exhaust the Republic, the Jedi become military commanders, Anakin Skywalker falls, and the Empire is born.

5

Reign of the Empire

The Imperial age. Jedi survivors hide, systems are occupied, clones are phased out, and resistance cells form. The Death Star project becomes the symbol of rule through overwhelming terror.

6

Age of Rebellion

The original trilogy era. Luke, Leia, Han, Chewbacca, Lando, droids, pilots, spies, and soldiers help transform scattered resistance into a military and moral challenge to the Empire.

7

New Republic and First Order

After Endor, the New Republic struggles to demilitarize and govern. Imperial remnants reorganize into the First Order. Rey, Finn, Poe, Rose, and Leia's Resistance confront a new authoritarian threat and an old Sith design.
